2012 MARRS 2 and 3 @ VIR in Alton, VA

The Mid-Atlantic Road Racing Series (MARRS) held its second and third regional SCCA Club Racing events of the 2012 season on Saturday May 12th and Sunday May 13th at VIR in Alton, Virginia. This was a joint SARRC event on one of their home tracks. This was the Cahall brother's second trip to VIR in 2012.

As with MARRS 1, Larry and Ted Cahall practiced on Friday and brother Bob joined them late Friday night for the two days of racing. Ted ran in three classes: SM, SM5, and STL. Bob and Larry each ran only in the SM class.

On Saturday Ted qualified 5th out of 6 cars in SM5. He also qualified 1st out of 2 cars for the STL class and 13th out of 24 cars in the SM class. Larry and Bob qualified 17th and 22nd for the SM class.

In the SM class Saturday race, Ted finished 8th, Larry 13th, and Bob finished 15th. Ted finished 1st in the very small STL race and 5th in the SM5 race.

On Sunday, in the SM class, Ted, Larry, and Bob finished 10th, 18th, and 19th respectively. Ted took first place (out of 2 cars) in STL and finished 5th out of 6 cars for the SM5 race.

    

As always, a huge thank you goes out to Jimmy, Sherise, Wendy, Karen, Mike Collins and Meathead Racing for their pit crew services!
