2011 SCCA National Championship Runoffs

Come watch Ted Cahall compete as the Sports Car Club of America (SCCA) holds its National Championships during the week of September 18th. This will be Ted's second season at the SCCA National Championship Runoffs.

Ted Cahall plans to participate with Mike Collins as one of the two cars from the Meathead Racing team based in Summit Point, WV.
