Ted Cahall SCCA Club Racing

Ted Cahall is a member of the Sports Car Club of America (SCCA) and holds an SCCA Club Racing National Competition License.  He plans to race in the SCCA National Championship Runoffs in 2019 at VIR. Ted has previously raced in the SCCA Club Racing National Championship Runoffs in 2010, 2011 and 2013.

He can be seen racing the #77 Spec Miata pictured and is a member of the Meathead Racing team. Ted races regionally in the Mid-Atlantic Road Racing Series (MARRS) as part of the Washington DC Region (WDCR). He finished 10th in the 2010 series 11th in 2011, 9th in 2012 and 5th in 2013. To better track his results, Ted created the marrspoints.com points tracking system for the Washington DC Region.

Ted's brother Larry Cahall also raced in the 2010 through 2013 seasons and their brother Bob Cahall joined them in 2011 for his rookie season.
