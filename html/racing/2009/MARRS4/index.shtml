Ted Cahall Completes MARRS 4, Gets Regional License!

Ted Cahall participated in the MARRS 4 SCCA race on May 31, 2009. This race was significant as it allowed Ted to earn his regional SCCA Club Racing Regional License.  Ted's fastest lap times were progressively faster from the Saturday morning qualifier session (1:35.625), to the qualification race (1:35.013), to the actual race on Sunday (1:34.260).

Sunday's fastest lap was Ted's fastest recorded time on the Summit Point "Main" Course.  Click here or on the photo below to see more photos from the MARRS 4 weekend.  You can also read about this event on Ted's blog post.
