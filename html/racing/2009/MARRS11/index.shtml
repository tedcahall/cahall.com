MARRS 11 Weekend, New Personal Best Lap

Ted Cahall participated in the SCCA MARRS 11 races on October 10th-11th weekend. Ted's fastest lap time on Saturday was a new personal best - with a 1:31.9.   His previous fastest lap was a 1:32.260 set on Monday of the Labor Day weekend races. On Sunday October 11th, the final race of the 2009 MARRS series of races for SCCA Club Racing, Ted was able to run a 1:31.8 and finished his season with his fastest lap ever.
