Ted Cahall Completes 2009 SCCA MARRS 1 Race
On Sunday April 19th, Ted Cahall participated in the SCCA MARRS 1 race.  The day was held on the Main Course at Summit Point.  Below is a photo of the car Ted purchased from "Bad" Al Bell.  Ted finished in position 32 of 44 racers.  There were a number of racers knocked out on the first lap.  The race is covered in detail on Ted Cahall's blog.  You can click here or on the photo below to see more pictures of the race.
