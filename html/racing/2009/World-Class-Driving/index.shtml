Ted Cahall Takes the World Class Driving Tour

Ted Cahall participated in the World Class Driving tour on May 21st 2009. The event featured five exotic cars and is covered on his blog. It was a fantastic day including rides in each of:
  • Audi R8
  • Callaway C16 Corvette
  • Lamborghini LP560
  • Ford GT
  • Alfa-Romeo 8C
