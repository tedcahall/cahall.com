Ted Cahall in the 2009 SCCA Club Trials
Ted Cahall participated in the SCCA Spring Club Trials in preparation for the MARRS Series in 2009.  Ted's fastest lap around the Shenandoah Course at Summit Point was 1:50 seconds.  The car below is the '99 SM class Spec Miata that I used that day and subsequently bought from Al Bell.  You can read about this event on my blog and see more pictures on Webshots.
