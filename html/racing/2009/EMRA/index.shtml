EMRA Sprints and 4 Hour Enduro

Ted Cahall participated in the EMRA Sprints and 4 Hour Enduro at Summit Point on November 14th.  These two races represent Ted's best finishes of his rookie season.  He finished 4th in a field of 9 SM Spec Miatas in the Sprints after missing a shift in turn 6 to let the car behind him squeak by.  He was 9th overall of all the small bore cars in a field of 22 cars.

Ted also combined with Bad Al Bell in the EMRA 4 Hour Enduro race to finish 3rd overall (and win his first trophy).  They were also 1st in the SM class  for the Enduro as well.  A huge thank you goes out to Al for his excellent driving skills and to Mike Collins and Meathead Racing for their pit crew services!  Click through on the picture below to see more photos of the event at Webshots.
