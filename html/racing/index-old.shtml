Ted Cahall SCCA Club Racing

Ted Cahall is a member of SCCA Club Racing and holds a National SCCA Club Racing License.  He attended the 2010 SCCA National Championship Runoffs on September 20th through 24th. He races a Spec Miata and is a proud member of the Meathead Racing team. There are a number of SCCA races with the Washington DC Region (WDCR) chapter of the SCCA that are part of the Mid-Atlantic Road Racing Series (MARRS).  The following pages include Ted's 2010 racing events as well as highlight of his 2009 and 2008 Events. Ted's brother Larry Cahall also raced in 2010!

Read more about Ted's experiences with the SCCA, his Spec Miata, and racing on his blog and watch some of his racing videos on YouTube.  Click on the links below the photo to see information on events Ted has attended.
