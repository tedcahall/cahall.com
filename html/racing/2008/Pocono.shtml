Ted Cahall in the 2008 AMG Challenge
Ted Cahall participated in the AMG Challenge at Pocono Raceway on September 18, 2008.  The photo below is from that event with AMG.  The car is the 2008 AMG SL65 "Black Series".  Lots of carbon fiber on this car.  Below the photo is the Certificate of Completion for the "Graduate" (advanced) Program.  I highly recommend this program to any drivers that want to get out on a real race course and drive all of the AMG models.  It is held in several locations across the U.S.
