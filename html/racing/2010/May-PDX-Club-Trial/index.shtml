May 2010 PDX and Club Trial - Shenandoah Circuit

Ted and his brother Larry attended the SCCA PDX and Club Trials on May 1st and 2nd. Larry got considerably faster over the course of the weekend but Ted failed to set a new personal course record for the Shenandoah Circuit with a fast lap of 1:48.6 against a personal best of 1:47. Larry came in at a 1:53 which is a very respectable time for a rookie with one race under his belt.
