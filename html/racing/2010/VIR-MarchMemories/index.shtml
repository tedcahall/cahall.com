VIR March Memories

Ted Cahall participated in the March Memories at VIR on March 5th through 7th (see photos on Webshots).  It was three great days of racing. Friday was a practice day to help racers tune their cars and get familiar with the track. Saturday was a set of qualifiers for the SARRC race and the Carolina Cup Series - plus the Carolina Cup race itself. Sunday included the SARRC race, the qualifiers for the Enduro race and the 1.5 hour Enduro. It was an action packed weekend with 11 total sessions on the "Full" VIR race track. Ted's best time was a 2:27.1 - which was considerably better than the 2:40+ he did on the Friday practice sessions.

As always, a huge thank you goes out to Mike Collins and Meathead Racing for their pit crew services!
