2010 VIR SCCA Double National

On Friday April 16th through Sunday April 18th, the Meathead Racing Team including: Mike Collins, Chris Collins, Neil O'Rourke, Ralph Dommes and Ted Cahall participated in the Double National race with SCCA Club Racing at Virginia International Raceway (VIR) in Alton, VA. Special Thanks to Karen Dildei for photos of the event.

Ted got progressively faster each day setting new personal best times of 2:26.9 on Friday, 2:25.5 on Saturday and a 2:24.9 on Sunday. These were Ted's first National SCCA races and there were drivers from across the United States in attendance. Mike finished 10th in class for Spec Miatas nationally on Sunday. Both Mike and Neil turned in 2:20 laps several times over the weekend. See video of the crash at the start of the race on Sunday.
