Ted Cahall 2014 SCCA Club Racing Schedule   

This is a page of all of the racing events Ted Cahall plans to attend in 2014.  All of these events are sponsored by the SCCA and the regional events are part of the Mid-Atlantic Road Racing Series (MARRS).  Ted is a member of the Meathead Racing team and holds an SCCA Club Racing National Competition License.  A couple of years ago Ted started working with a professional racing coach, Todd Lamb.  Read more about Ted's experiences with the SCCA, his Spec Miata, and racing on his blog.   See some racing videos of Ted on YouTube!
