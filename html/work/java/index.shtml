Ted Cahall and James Gosling discuss Java

Ted is a huge fan of the Java programming language. It is free, object oriented, and available on almost every imaginable platform including Linux. Below is a picture of Ted Cahall with James Gosling, the inventor of Java. The photo was taken in May of 2000 at the CNET studio in San Francisco when CNET was converting their publishing systems to Java. It was great of James to stop by to speak to the employees and field questions.
