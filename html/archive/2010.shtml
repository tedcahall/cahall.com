Ted Cahall and his brother Larry participated in the final 2010 MARRS series race on October 9th and 10th. Ted had his second top ten finish of the year. His points for this race put him in 10th place in the overall MARRS point standings for 2010. Both Ted and Larry set personal best lap times over the weekend.

Ted Cahall joined the Meathead Racing team at Road America in Elkhart Lake, WI September 18th through 24th for the 2010 SCCA National Championship Runoffs. Running from Meathead Racing in addition to Ted were: Mike Collins, Tim Jacobs, Neil O'Rourke, Chris Collins and David Jacobs. Ted finished 33rd in a field of 63 cars while Tim and Mike finished 19th and 20th respectively.

Meathead Racing was in prime form over Labor Day with Mike Collins taking first place in all three races and Tim Jacobs setting the track record twice!!! Larry and Ted Cahall also participated in MARSS 9 and 10 for the Labor Day Double race. They both set new personal fastest lap times and Ted had his first MARRS top ten finish on Saturday.
