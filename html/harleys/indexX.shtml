Ted Cahall's Harley-Davidson Motorcycles

Ted has owned three Harley-Davidson motorcycles: a heavily modified Sportster (seen in this photo), his current 2000 Fat Boy (FLSTF) (below) and a more recently purchased 2009 Screamin' Eagle Road Glide (right).  He has done some serious engine modifications to his Fat Boy including: larger cylinders and pistons (96 ci), 43mm Mikuni Carburetor, a fairly hot cam, Vance & Hines Pipes and a bunch of chrome.

The photo below left is with a number of co-workers from AOL while they were down at the 2008 Myrtle Beach Bike Week.  Ted is the guy on the far right propping up Dave in the black t-shirt.  The photo directly to the right of that photo is also of a number of AOL co-workers from the 2009 Myrtle Beach Bike Week trip. You can read Ted's blog about their ride down to Bike Week for 2009.
