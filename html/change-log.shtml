Recent Changes:

Ted had his best finish of the year at the EMRA Sprints (4th in class) and 4 Hour Enduro (1st in class) on November 14th.  You can read about it here and on his blog.  On September 7th, Ted completed his 6th SCCA race and earned his National SCCA Club Racing license.  Previously, on May 31st, Ted completed his second SCCA Regional race, MARRS 4, and earned his Regional SCCA Club Racing License. You can see photos on Webshots, and read about obtaining his regional license on his blog.  You  can also see some racing videos of Ted on YouTube.

Ted Cahall joined the Bertil Roos racing school up in the Poconos for a little Formula 2000 instruction on October 7th.

Ted joined Rolling Thunder in DC on Memorial Day weekend and blogged about it with photos and videos.  You can also read about Ted's participation in the "World Class Driving Tour" on his blog (middle and right photo below).  The event included driving five exotic sports cars: Lamborghini LP560, Callaway C16 Corvette, Ford GT, Audi R8, and an Alfa-Romeo 8C.
