About Ted Cahall

Ted Cahall lives in the Minneapolis, MN area after spending the previous three plus years in the Seattle, WA area and the four years before that in Northern Virginia. On sunny weekends you can often find Ted riding his motorcycles,  driving his Corvettes or racing his Spec Miata with Meathead Racing in the SCCA. In the winter, he can be found on his snowmobiles.
