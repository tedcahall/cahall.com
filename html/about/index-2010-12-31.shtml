About Ted Cahall

Ted Cahall lives in the Bellevue, WA area after spending the previous four years in Northern Virginia where he served on the boards of a number of local NoVA business councils as well as non-profits. On sunny weekends you can often find Ted riding his motorcycles,  driving his Corvettes or racing his Spec Miata with the SCCA. In the winter, he can be found on his snowmobiles.  A listing of some of his photo albums on Webshots can be found here.
